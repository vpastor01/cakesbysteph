Privacy Policy

Cakes by Steph LLC ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit cakesbystephva.com or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website, you agree to this policy.

Last updated: June 2026.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit a tasting, custom-order, contact, or other form, we collect the details you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, event date, and any notes about your cake or event.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we collect standard analytics data such as pages visited, device and browser type, and general location, through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry, prepare a quote, and plan and fulfill your order.
  • To contact you about your request by phone, text, or email.
  • To understand and improve our website and our marketing.

Service providers we use

We share information only as needed with the services that help us run the business:

  • Netlify hosts the website and processes form submissions.
  • Twilio is used to send Stephanie a text alert with your inquiry details so she can follow up quickly.
  • Google Analytics and Google Ads help us measure website traffic and the performance of our advertising. These services may set cookies and collect usage data subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising measurement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended. You can also opt out of Google Analytics using Google's browser add-on, and manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.

Data retention and security

We keep your inquiry details only as long as needed to serve you and for our reasonable business records, and we take sensible steps to protect the information we hold.

Your choices

You may ask us what information we hold about you, request that we correct or delete it, or ask us to stop contacting you. Just reach out using the details below.
