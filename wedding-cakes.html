Questions, answered

How far in advance should we book?

Weekend dates book four to six weeks out and fill first in peak season. Reach out early so your date is held and there is time to design without a rush.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes. Every wedding begins with a private tasting for $55, where you choose flavors and talk through your vision. That fee credits toward your booked cake.

How do you size the cake for our guest count?

Tell us your final headcount and Stephanie tiers the cake to serve it, with clean portions and a scale that suits your venue and table.

Do you deliver and set up at our venue?

Yes. We hand-deliver in climate-controlled transport and assemble on site across Leesburg, Ashburn, Sterling, and the wider Northern Virginia area.

Are the cakes made from scratch?

Always. Real butter, farm-fresh eggs, Madagascar vanilla, and Valrhona chocolate, baked by hand. Share any dietary needs at your tasting and we plan around them.
