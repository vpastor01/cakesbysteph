Wedding Cakes

A centerpiece designed for your day, your aesthetic, and your guest count. Floral, minimalist, sculptural, or opulent.

Your wedding cake should look like it belongs only to your wedding. Stephanie starts from a blank canvas and designs around your colors, your venue, and the feeling you want the room to hold when the cake is revealed.

Every tier is baked from scratch and finished by hand. You taste and choose your flavors at a private tasting before a single decision is locked, so the cake on your table is exactly the one you fell for.

What Your Commission Includes

  • A private tasting to choose flavors and direction
  • A custom design built around your theme and venue
  • Hand-baked tiers sized to your guest count
  • White-glove delivery and on-site setup
  • A single point of contact, Stephanie, start to finish

One Artist

Stephanie designs, bakes, and delivers your cake herself. No delegation, from sketch to table.

From Scratch

Real butter, farm-fresh eggs, Madagascar vanilla, Valrhona chocolate. No mixes, ever.

White-Glove Delivery

Climate-controlled transport and on-site assembly, presented exactly as designed.
