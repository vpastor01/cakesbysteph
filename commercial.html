Questions

Wholesale & standing orders

Is there a minimum order for wholesale and standing orders?

Yes. We set a minimum per delivery based on the items and how often you order, and we confirm it in your quote before anything is scheduled.

Where do you deliver?

We deliver across Northern Virginia, including Leesburg, Ashburn, Sterling, and the wider Loudoun County area. Tell us your location and we will confirm delivery for your address.

How much lead time do you need?

Recurring orders run on a schedule we set with you up front. New wholesale accounts usually start within one to two weeks of the first consultation.

How does billing work?

We invoice every order and payment is due at delivery. New accounts are quick to set up, so you can start ordering right away.

Can we change quantities on a standing order?

Yes. You can adjust quantities ahead of each delivery with reasonable notice, so your order tracks your actual volume.
