Corporate & Event Cake FAQs

How far in advance should I order a corporate or event cake?

Reach out as early as you can. Branded and showpiece cakes are typically booked three to four weeks ahead, and sooner is better for large events or busy seasons.

Can you match our logo and brand colors?

Yes. We design cakes in your brand colors and reproduce logos and branding as part of the commission, so the cake looks like an extension of your company.

How do you size a cake for our headcount?

Tell us your guest count and we design the cake and any dessert tables to serve it, with clean portions and a presentation that fits the room.

Do you deliver and set up at our venue?

Yes. We hand-deliver and set up on site across Northern Virginia, so the centerpiece is presented exactly as designed.

Can you handle dessert tables and individual servings, not just a cake?

Yes. Alongside the main cake we build dessert tables and individually portioned desserts for the event, all made from scratch.
