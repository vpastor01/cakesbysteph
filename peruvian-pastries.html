Peruvian Pastries

Authentic alfajores, tres leches, and milhojas, made with reverence for the tradition.

Stephanie trained under a Peruvian pastry master, and her heritage collection honors that craft. Alfajores with delicate manjar blanco, tres leches soaked just right, milhojas built in crisp layers.

Each pastry is made from scratch with the same ingredients and care as every cake. The collection suits celebrations, gifts, and anyone who grew up with these flavors and wants the real thing.
