Questions, answered

Which Peruvian pastries do you make?

Alfajores, milhojas, pionono, empanadas, tres leches, and more, all baked from scratch with authentic recipes.

Can I order by the dozen?

Yes. Pastries are available by the dozen, and you can mix an assortment for an event or send a box as a gift.

How much notice do you need?

Give as much notice as you can, especially for events and large orders. Reach out and we will confirm what works for your date.

Do you deliver?

Yes. Orders can be picked up or delivered across Leesburg, Ashburn, Sterling, and the wider Northern Virginia area.

Is everything made from scratch?

Always. Every pastry is made to order by hand, never from a mix.
