Wedding Cake Trends for 2026

Trends are a starting point, not a rulebook, but they are a fun way to find your direction. Here is what we are loving for 2026 weddings.

Textured buttercream

Hand-combed and sculptural textures are everywhere, organic, tactile, and beautifully photogenic.

Pressed and fresh flowers

Pressed edible flowers and fresh florals bring color and a garden feel, especially lovely for Loudoun's vineyard weddings.

Bold color and timeless white

Two ends of the spectrum are both having a moment: confident color, and crisp, timeless white done impeccably.

Choosing what lasts

The best cake reflects you and your day, not the calendar. See wedding cakes.

Frequently asked questions

What are the wedding cake trends for 2026?

Textured buttercream, pressed and fresh florals, bold color, and timeless white done impeccably.

Are tiered cakes still in style?

Yes, classic tiers remain timeless; the finish and detail are what feel current.

How do I pick a style that won't feel dated?

Start from what reflects you; a well-made classic always lasts.
