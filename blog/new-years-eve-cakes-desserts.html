New Year's Eve Cakes & Desserts in Northern Virginia

New Year's Eve is the one night made for a little glamour. Here are ideas to make the dessert as celebratory as the countdown, plus a reminder to order before the holiday rush.

Glamorous designs

Gold accents, metallic detail, sparkle, and deep color all suit the night. A tall, dramatic cake makes a striking centerpiece for a party.

Party desserts

For a crowd, a cake plus a dessert table of individual servings and pastries keeps the night moving. A box of Peruvian pastries adds something special.

Order before the holidays

Late December is busy from start to finish, so reach out before the holiday week to hold your date.

Frequently asked questions

What is a good New Year's Eve cake?

Gold and metallic detail, sparkle, and a tall, dramatic silhouette for a party centerpiece.

Can you do desserts for a New Year's party?

Yes, a cake plus a dessert table of individual servings and pastries.

How early should I order?

Before the holiday week, since late December books up fast.
