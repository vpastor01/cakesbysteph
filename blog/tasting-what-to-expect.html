What to Expect at Your Private Tasting

The private tasting is the first real step of every commission, and it does more than let you sample flavors. It is where Stephanie learns your celebration, your taste, and the feeling you want the day to carry.

Plan to spend about an hour. You will taste a selection of from-scratch cakes and fillings, talk through your colors and theme, and look at directions for the design. Bring photos, fabric swatches, or anything that captures the look you love.

By the end, you will have chosen your flavors and a clear design direction. The $55 tasting fee credits toward your booked commission, so it becomes part of the cake itself.

Come with questions. Stephanie would rather answer twenty than leave one detail to chance.
