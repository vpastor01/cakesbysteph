Valentine's Day Cakes & Desserts in Northern Virginia

Valentine's is one of the sweetest weekends of the year, and one of the fastest to book. Whether it is a cake for two or a dessert table for a party, here are ideas and the one piece of timing advice that matters most: order early.

Ideas for the day

  • Heart cakes — the classic, in romantic palettes with piping or hand-painted detail.
  • Dessert boxes — a curated mix of treats to gift or share.
  • For two — a small, special cake rather than a crowd-sized one.
  • Peruvian pastries — alfajores and milhojas make a lovely, less-expected gift. See Peruvian pastries.

Flavors

Everything is from scratch, so flavor leads. Chocolate, vanilla bean, fruit, or dulce de leche all suit the day. Choose yours at the tasting, or tell us your favorite when you order.

Order early

Valentine's weekend fills quickly, so reach out a week or two ahead to be sure of a spot. Stephanie personally replies within 24 hours to confirm your date.

Frequently asked questions

Do you make heart-shaped cakes for Valentine's Day?

Yes, heart cakes in romantic palettes with piping or hand-painted detail, sized for two or for a party.

How early should I order a Valentine's cake?

A week or two ahead is wise, as the weekend books up fast.

Do you have gift options besides cake?

Yes, dessert boxes and Peruvian pastries like alfajores make lovely Valentine's gifts.
