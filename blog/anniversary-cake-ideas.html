Anniversary Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

An anniversary cake is a quiet kind of romantic. It can echo the day you started, or simply mark another good year. Here are a few directions, for a milestone party or a table for two.

Recreate your wedding cake

A favorite for anniversaries: a smaller version of your original wedding cake, same flavors, same feeling. Bring a photo and Stephanie recreates the look. See wedding cakes.

Milestone designs

For a 25th or 50th, gold and silver accents and elegant florals suit the occasion. For any year, a refined single tier is plenty.

Keep it intimate

Not every anniversary needs a big cake. A small, special dessert for two is often exactly right.

Frequently asked questions

Can you recreate our wedding cake for an anniversary?

Yes. Share a photo and Stephanie recreates the look and flavors at the size you need.

What is a good 25th or 50th anniversary cake?

Elegant florals with gold or silver accents read beautifully for milestone anniversaries.

How far ahead should I order?

Two to three weeks is comfortable, earlier for busy weekends.
