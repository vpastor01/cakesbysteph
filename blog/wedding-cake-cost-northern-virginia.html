How Much Does a Wedding Cake Cost in Northern Virginia?

It is the first question almost every couple asks, and the honest answer is that a custom wedding cake is priced to your celebration, not to a catalog. Two cakes that serve the same number of guests can sit at very different prices depending on the design. Here is how that math actually works, so you can plan with confidence.

What drives the price

A handful of factors shape every quote:

  • Guest count. Cakes are sized and tiered to serve your final headcount, so servings are the foundation of the price.
  • Design complexity. Hand-piped detail, sugar flowers, hand-painting, and sculptural work take time, and time is most of the cost in a from-scratch cake.
  • Tiers and structure. Taller, multi-tier cakes need internal support and more finishing than a single tier.
  • Flavors and fillings. Premium ingredients, layered fillings, and special dietary work can shift the price.
  • Delivery and setup. White-glove delivery and on-site assembly across Northern Virginia are part of the commission, so the cake arrives exactly as designed.

What you can expect to budget

At Cakes by Steph, custom cakes start at $195, and wedding cakes are quoted to your guest count and design rather than a flat package. The most reliable way to think about it is per serving: as the design grows more detailed, the per-serving price rises with it. Sharing your headcount, venue, and a few inspiration photos is enough for a clear, specific quote.

How to get an accurate quote

Every commission begins with a private tasting for $55, and that fee credits toward your booked cake. At the tasting you choose flavors, talk through colors and design, and leave with a clear direction. From there you receive a quote built around your real numbers, not an estimate.

A 50% deposit holds your date, with the balance due about a week before the wedding. Weekend dates book four to six weeks out and fill first in peak season, so it is worth reaching out early.

Frequently asked questions

How is a custom wedding cake priced?

By the number of servings and the complexity of the design. Tiers, hand-piped detail, sugar flowers, and finishes all factor in, along with delivery and on-site setup.

Is there a starting price?

Custom cakes start at $195, and wedding cakes are quoted to your guest count and design. You receive a clear quote with no obligation.

Does the tasting cost extra?

The private tasting is $55, and that fee credits toward your booked cake.
