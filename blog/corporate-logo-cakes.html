Branded & Logo Cakes for Corporate Events

A branded cake turns a corporate moment into a photo, and a photo into reach. For launches, openings, milestones, and client events, here is how a logo or branded cake comes together, and the options for larger gatherings.

Logo and branded designs

Your logo can be reproduced in edible print or hand-rendered, matched to your brand colors. The result is a clean, professional showpiece that looks right in the room and on camera. See the corporate cakes page.

Dessert tables and individual servings

For bigger crowds, a branded cake can anchor a dessert table of cupcakes, individual servings, and pastries, so everyone is served without an oversized cake. We also cater openings and client celebrations across Northern Virginia.

Standing and wholesale orders

Restaurants, cafés, and offices can set up recurring orders with priority scheduling and one dedicated contact. See commercial & wholesale for details.

Planning and lead time

For dated corporate events, reach out as early as you can, especially for larger orders. One point of contact handles your account start to finish.

Frequently asked questions

Can you put our company logo on a cake?

Yes, in edible print or hand-rendered, matched to your brand colors.

Do you cater corporate events and offices?

Yes. Branded cakes, dessert tables, and individual servings for launches, milestones, and client gatherings across Northern Virginia.

Can we set up a recurring order?

Yes. Standing and wholesale orders run on a schedule with priority slots and a dedicated contact.
