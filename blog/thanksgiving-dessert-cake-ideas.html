Thanksgiving Dessert & Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

Thanksgiving is about the table, and dessert gets to be the happy finish. Here are warm, autumnal ideas, from a centerpiece cake to a spread that gives everyone a choice.

Fall flavors

Warm, seasonal flavors shine: spiced cake, dulce de leche, and tres leches all suit the day. Everything is from scratch, so the flavor is the point, not just the look.

A dessert spread for the family

Instead of one dessert, a small cake plus an assortment lets everyone choose. A box of Peruvian pastries adds something a little different to the usual lineup.

Order ahead of the week

Thanksgiving week is busy, so order two to three weeks ahead to be sure of your spot. Stephanie replies the same day to confirm.

Frequently asked questions

What desserts do you make for Thanksgiving?

Spiced and seasonal cakes, tres leches, and an assortment of pastries to round out the table.

Can you do a dessert table for a big family dinner?

Yes, a centerpiece cake plus an assortment so everyone has a choice.

How early should I order for Thanksgiving?

Two to three weeks ahead, since the week books up.
