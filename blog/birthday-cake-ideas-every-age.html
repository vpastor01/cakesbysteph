Custom Birthday Cake Ideas for Every Age

A birthday cake should feel like the person it is for. Whether that is a cartoon-bright kid's cake or a quiet, elegant milestone, here are ideas by age and a few things that make a custom birthday cake special.

For kids

Bold colors, favorite characters, and a theme they will light up over. Hand-shaped toppers, fondant figures, and bright buttercream all work. Tell us the character or theme and Stephanie brings it to life.

For teens and milestones

Sweet 16s and graduations call for something that photographs well: drip cakes, gold accents, florals, or a sleek modern look. See the Sweet 16 ideas for more.

For adults

Elegant and understated, or a fun nod to a hobby or favorite thing, with hand-painted detail, refined palettes, and a flavor that is genuinely good.

What makes a custom birthday cake different

Everything is baked from scratch and designed around one person, so it tastes as considered as it looks. See the birthday cakes page for examples.

Frequently asked questions

Do you make character or themed cakes?

Yes, themed and character birthday cakes for all ages, designed around what they love.

How far ahead should I order a birthday cake?

Two to three weeks is comfortable for most birthday cakes; earlier for elaborate designs or busy weekends.

Can you match a specific theme or color?

Yes. Share the theme, colors, and any inspiration photos, and the cake is designed around them.
