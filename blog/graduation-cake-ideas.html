Graduation Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

Graduation season is a happy rush, and a cake is the centerpiece of the party. Here are ideas that celebrate the grad, and a reminder that May and June fill quickly.

Design ideas

  • School colors — matched to the cap and gown.
  • Photo or name cakes — a printed photo or the grad's name and year.
  • Dessert tables — a cake plus cupcakes and pastries for a crowd.

Sizing for the party

Grad parties can be large. A cake for the photo moment plus a dessert table or sheet cake for the back of the house serves everyone. Stephanie sizes it to your headcount.

Book early for grad season

May and June are among the busiest weeks of the year. Reach out as soon as you have a date so it is held.

Frequently asked questions

Can you make a graduation cake in school colors?

Yes, matched to the cap and gown, with the grad's name, year, or photo if you like.

How do I serve a big graduation party?

A centerpiece cake plus a dessert table or sheet cake for the back of the room serves a crowd cleanly.

How early should I order?

As early as you can, since May and June book up fast.
