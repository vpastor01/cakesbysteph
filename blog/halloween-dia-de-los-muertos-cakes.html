Halloween & Día de los Muertos Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

October brings two beautiful, very different celebrations. Here are ideas for both, whether you want playful and spooky or warm and meaningful.

Halloween designs

Playful for kids, elegant-eerie for adults: deep colors, drips, hand-painted detail, and themed toppers. As bright or as moody as you like.

Día de los Muertos

For Día de los Muertos, designs draw on marigold orange, papel picado patterns, and ofrenda-inspired detail, a warm, celebratory tribute. Pair it with Peruvian pastries for the table.

Timing

Two to three weeks of notice is comfortable, more for detailed designs. Reach out and Stephanie confirms within 24 hours.

Frequently asked questions

Do you make Halloween cakes?

Yes, from playful kids' designs to elegant, moody cakes for adults.

Can you make a Día de los Muertos cake?

Yes, with marigold colors, papel picado patterns, and ofrenda-inspired detail.

How far ahead should I order?

Two to three weeks, earlier for elaborate designs.
