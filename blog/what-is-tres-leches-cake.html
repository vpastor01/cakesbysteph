What Is Tres Leches Cake?

Tres leches is one of those desserts people fall for at the first bite. Here is what it is, why it is so loved, and how to order one for your gathering.

The three milks

Tres leches, “three milks,” is a sponge cake soaked in a blend of evaporated milk, condensed milk, and cream until it is impossibly moist, then topped with whipped cream. Light, rich, and not too sweet, it is beloved across Latin America.

Why people love it

The soak makes it tender in a way no other cake is, and it works beautifully as a centerpiece or alongside a dessert table. It pairs wonderfully with Peruvian pastries like alfajores.

Ordering a tres leches cake

Made from scratch and available for events and gatherings. Reach out with your date and guest count and we will plan it.

Frequently asked questions

What is tres leches cake?

A sponge cake soaked in three milks, evaporated, condensed, and cream, topped with whipped cream.

Is tres leches very sweet?

It is rich but balanced, not overly sweet, which is part of why it is so popular.

Can you make tres leches for an event?

Yes, from scratch, sized to your gathering.
