Easter & Spring Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

Spring is the prettiest season for a cake, all soft color and fresh flowers. For Easter and the first warm gatherings, here are gentle ideas and a note on booking the holiday weekend.

Design ideas

  • Soft pastels — the signature look of spring.
  • Sugar or fresh flowers — for a garden feel.
  • Hand-painted detail — delicate and one of a kind.

Easter brunch and gatherings

For a brunch, a smaller cake paired with an assortment of pastries gives everyone a choice. A box of Peruvian pastries adds something a little different to the table.

Reserve early

The Easter weekend books up, so reach out early to hold your date. Stephanie personally replies within 24 hours.

Frequently asked questions

What is a good Easter cake?

Soft pastels with sugar or fresh flowers, or a hand-painted spring design.

Can you do desserts for an Easter brunch?

Yes, a smaller cake plus an assortment of pastries works beautifully for brunch.

How early should I order?

Early in the week before, since the holiday weekend fills up.
