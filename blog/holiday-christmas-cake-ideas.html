Holiday & Christmas Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

The holidays are the busiest, warmest stretch of the year, and a beautiful cake or a box of pastries makes the table. Here are festive ideas, and the timing to keep in mind for December.

Festive designs

  • Classic Christmas — red, green, and gold with vintage piping.
  • Elegant winter — whites, soft metallics, and frosted detail.
  • Feliz Navidad — festive cakes for the family gathering.

Party desserts and gifts

For gatherings, a cake plus a dessert table works beautifully, and a box of Peruvian pastries like alfajores makes a lovely, less-expected holiday gift.

Order early for December

December fills fast, especially the weekends around the holidays. Reach out early so your date is held, and Stephanie replies within 24 hours.

Frequently asked questions

Do you make Christmas and holiday cakes?

Yes, festive designs in classic red-green-gold or elegant winter whites, made from scratch.

Do you have holiday gift options?

Yes. Boxes of Peruvian pastries like alfajores and milhojas make lovely holiday gifts.

How early should I order for December?

As early as you can, since the holiday weekends book up quickly.
