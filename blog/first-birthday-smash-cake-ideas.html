First Birthday & Smash Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

A first birthday is really a party for everyone who made it through the first year. The classic setup is a sweet party cake plus a small smash cake just for the baby. Here is how to plan it.

The party cake and smash cake combo

One cake for the guests and a small, simple smash cake for the baby to dig into, photos guaranteed. Both can match the same gentle theme.

Gentle themes

Soft pastels, garden and animal themes, and a name or “ONE” topper all suit the day. See the baby & baptism cakes page for the feel.

A note for little ones

If you would like the smash cake made with simpler ingredients or around a dietary need, just ask, see our note on dietary cakes.

Frequently asked questions

What is a smash cake?

A small, simple cake just for the birthday baby to dig into, alongside the larger cake for guests.

Can you match the smash cake to the party cake?

Yes, both can share the same theme and colors.

Can the smash cake accommodate dietary needs?

Often, yes. Tell us what you need and we plan around it.
