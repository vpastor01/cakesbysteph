Baby Shower & Baptism Cake Ideas in Northern Virginia

A baby shower or baptism cake should feel as gentle as the moment it celebrates. These are soft, tender occasions, and the cake gets to be pretty, personal, and just sweet enough. Here are ideas and the practical details for planning one in Northern Virginia.

Design ideas by occasion

  • Baby showers — soft pastels, hand-piped detail, a name or due-date plaque.
  • Gender reveals — a neutral exterior with a colored surprise inside, or a tasteful palette.
  • Christenings & baptisms — clean whites, delicate piping, a cross or floral motif.
  • Welcome home & first birthdays — gentle themes that photograph beautifully.

See examples on the baby & baptism cakes page.

Sizing for your guest count

Showers and christenings are often intimate, so a single tier or a modest two-tier cake serves most gatherings. For larger family celebrations, a dessert table or a sheet cake for the back of the room serves everyone without an oversized centerpiece.

Flavors and dietary needs

Everything is made from scratch, so flavors are yours to choose at the tasting. Share any dietary needs and we plan around them, see our note on gluten-free and dairy-free cakes.

Timing

Two to three weeks of notice is comfortable for most shower and baptism cakes. Every commission begins with a private tasting for $55, credited toward your booked cake.

Frequently asked questions

How big should a baby shower cake be?

Most showers are served well by a single tier or a small two-tier cake; for larger gatherings, add a dessert table or sheet cake. Stephanie sizes it to your guest count.

Can you do a gender reveal cake?

Yes. The cake can hide a colored surprise inside, or use a tasteful blue or pink palette outside, whatever keeps your reveal the way you want it.

How far ahead should I order?

Two to three weeks is a good guide for shower and baptism cakes. Reach out earlier for busy weekends.
