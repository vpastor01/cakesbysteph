The Artist

Meet Stephanie

One artist, one set of hands, and a standard she refuses to lower.

Cakes by Steph began with a simple conviction: a celebration cake should be made by the person whose name is on it. Stephanie designs, bakes, and delivers every commission herself. No apprentices, no delegation, no shortcuts.

Her foundation is classical. Stephanie trained under a Peruvian pastry master, learning the discipline behind alfajores, tres leches, and milhojas, and carrying that same precision into every wedding tier and birthday centerpiece she builds today.

Ingredients as Philosophy

Real butter. Farm-fresh eggs. Madagascar vanilla. Valrhona chocolate. Stephanie sources with the care a fine kitchen demands and bakes everything from scratch. No mixes, ever. The difference is something you taste before you can name it.

Made for One Occasion

Nothing comes from a catalog. Every commission starts from a blank canvas and a private tasting, designed to reflect your vision and the moment it celebrates. To protect that level of care, Stephanie accepts a limited number of commissions each month.

She works from Leesburg and serves families across Loudoun County and Northern Virginia, with 47 five-star reviews from people who came back to say the cake was the thing everyone remembered.
