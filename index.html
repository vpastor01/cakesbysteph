Questions, answered

How far in advance should I order?

Weekend dates book four to six weeks out. Reach out as early as you can, especially for weddings and large events.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes. A private tasting is $55, and that amount is credited toward your order when you book.

Do you deliver?

We hand-deliver and set up across Leesburg, Ashburn, Sterling, and the wider Northern Virginia area.

How is pricing determined?

Every order is custom, so price depends on size, design, and detail. Share your vision and you receive a clear quote within 24 hours.

Is everything made from scratch?

Always. Every cake and pastry is baked by hand by Stephanie, never from a box or a mix.

Do you make Peruvian pastries?

Yes. Empanadas, alfajores, milhojas, pionono, and more, all authentic and made to order.
