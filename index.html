Meet Stephanie

One artist, one set of hands, one standard

Cakes by Steph was built on a simple promise: the person whose name is on the cake is the person making it. Stephanie designs, bakes, and delivers every commission herself.

Trained under a Peruvian pastry master, she brings the same discipline and precision to alfajores, milhojas, wedding tiers, and celebration cakes alike.

She works from Leesburg and serves clients across Loudoun County and Northern Virginia, accepting a limited number of commissions to protect quality and care.

Before you inquire

A few quick answers to the questions we hear most.

How far in advance should I order?

Weekend dates often book 4 to 6 weeks ahead. Weddings and larger events should reach out as early as possible.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes. A private tasting is $55, and that amount is credited toward your order when you book.

Do you deliver?

Yes. Orders are hand-delivered and set up across Leesburg, Ashburn, Sterling, and the wider Northern Virginia area.

How is pricing determined?

Every order is custom, so pricing depends on size, servings, design, and level of detail. The fastest way to get started is to send your date, guest count, and inspiration.
