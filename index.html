The Atelier Standard

Why Discerning Clients
Choose the Atelier

One Artist. Every Commission.

Stephanie personally designs, bakes, and delivers every creation. No apprentices. No delegation. Your cake receives her full artistry from sketch to table.

Ingredients as Philosophy

Real butter, farm-fresh eggs, Madagascar vanilla, Valrhona chocolate. We source with the same care a Michelin kitchen demands. No mixes. Ever.

Limited Monthly Commissions

To preserve quality, Stephanie accepts a select number of commissions each month. Weekends book 4–6 weeks out. Reserve early.

Bespoke — Not from a Catalog

No templates. Every commission is designed from a blank canvas, sculpted to reflect your unique vision and the occasion it celebrates.

White-Glove Delivery

Your commission arrives in climate-controlled transport, hand-assembled on-site, and presented exactly as designed. No exceptions.

Peruvian Heritage

Trained under a Peruvian pastry master, Stephanie brings authentic traditions to her heritage collection — made with reverence for the craft.
