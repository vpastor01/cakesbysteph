Birthday Cakes

Kids, adults, and milestones. Every birthday deserves a cake that feels like it was made just for them, because it was.

Whether it is a first birthday or a fiftieth, Stephanie designs a cake around the person it celebrates. Their favorite colors, their theme, their flavors, none of it pulled from a catalog.

Every birthday cake is baked from scratch and finished by hand. You choose flavors and direction at a tasting, so the surprise is the design, never the taste.
