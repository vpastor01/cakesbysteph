Quinceañera Cakes

Our signature specialty. Princess designs, elaborate tiers, and the details that make the moment unforgettable.

A quinceañera cake carries the whole spirit of the celebration. Stephanie designs tall, detailed centerpieces that match the gown, the colors, and the theme, built to hold the room's attention.

Each cake is baked from scratch and finished by hand, with the structural care that elaborate tiers demand. You taste and approve every flavor at your private tasting first.

What Your Commission Includes

  • A private tasting to choose flavors and design
  • Multi-tier structural designs built to your theme
  • Color and detail matched to the gown and decor
  • Hand-baked, from scratch, sized to your guest count
  • White-glove delivery and on-site assembly
